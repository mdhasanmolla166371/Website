About Monipur

Monipur is a historic village located in Homna Thana under Cumilla District, Chattogram Division, Bangladesh. The village is known for its rich cultural heritage, educational institutions, and strong community bonds. With numerous mosques, schools, and madrasas, Monipur stands as a model of community development in rural Bangladesh.

Thana: Homna
District: Cumilla
Division: Chattogram
